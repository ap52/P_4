Abbot Elementary

It had been a while since I physically laughed out loud because of a show, but Abbot Elementary delivers in that aspect! The only issues I really have with it is how short it is. I see a lot of people compare this to the Office or Parcs and Rec, and such comparisons are understandable. This show I feel has great potential to become one of the greats! 4.8/5

It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia

A show that has almost 15 seasons under its belt! (note I have not watched 14 or 15) I always find myself going back to this show, it is very re-watchable. The jokes thought I would consider very "edgy", but if you understand we are meant to be laughing at the characters the excellent writing shines throught! 4.9/5
