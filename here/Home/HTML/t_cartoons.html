BoJack Horseman

An adult cartoon show that focuses on the great character, BoJack Horseman. A terrible person, that the audience knows they're terrible. It is a great show, with many animal puns and jokes,but it is also a great drama, with an engaging story. It's not just a silly talking animal show, there are many themes that are clearly played out thought the show. 5/5
