Bridgerton

Season 1 was not that great. Season 2 on the other hand...it a great improvement! It was clear they wanted to attract Pride and Prejudice fans with seasons 2! THe thing about this show, that I praise highly for it, is its diversity. This kind of representation is important in all kinds of media. In a popular show like Bridgerton, it is extremely important that those who are underrepresented get their time to shine! It's not the greatest thing I've ever watched, it definitely has its issues, but it's a trashy fantasy that does what it had intended to do. 3/5
