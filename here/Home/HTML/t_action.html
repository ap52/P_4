Game of Thrones

If this was a review for only seasons 1-5, then I would say this is a masterpiece of a show with smart and excellent writing. However, that is not the case. Looking at the show as a whole, it obviously lost its quality over time, particular in season 8. That season is sort of infamously bad, which is a shame because I both love and hate this show. Watching the first early seasons was a great television viewing experience. It is a shame that it met such a steep decline of good writing, making it obvious D&D (GOT writers), relied heavily on the source material. 4/5
